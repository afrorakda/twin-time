# Twin Time (Fridge Combo #40)

**Twin Time** is a minimalist "Dual-Zone Clock" designed for remote workers, travelers, and global collaborators. Instead of cluttering the screen with multiple world clocks, this tool focuses on the only two people that matter: **You** and **Them**.

---

### **The "Empathy-First" Logic**
Traditional clocks treat all time zones equally. **Twin Time** prioritizes the person you are communicating with:

* **Them-Centric UI:** The target time is displayed in a massive, high-contrast font. You can see their time in a split second, even with a side-glance.
* **Night Indicator:** If it's night-time for them (8 PM – 5 AM), the interface automatically flips to a dark "Night Mode" with a 🌙 icon. It’s a visual "Stop" sign to prevent you from sending that late-night message.
* **Date Sync:** Instantly see if they are living in "Yesterday" or "Tomorrow" with clear relative date tags.

---

### **Features**
* **1 Action Setup:** Select their city once, and it’s saved forever (LocalStorage).
* **Auto-Locate:** Your time is automatically synced from your system clock.
* **Zero Decision Fatigue:** Only 10 major global hubs + manual UTC offsets. No searching through endless city lists.
* **Privacy-First:** Browser-only. No tracking, no account, no ads.

---

### **How to Use**
1.  Open the tool.
2.  Select the city or UTC offset of the person you're working with.
3.  Instantly see the "Time Gap" and their current environment.
4.  Add to your Home Screen for one-tap access.

---

### **Project Constitution**
* **1 Tool / 1 Action:** Solves the "What time is it there?" problem instantly.
* **Design:** High-impact bold typography, 15px rounded corners, #333 Main.
* **Privacy:** No login / No tracking / No ads / Browser-only.
